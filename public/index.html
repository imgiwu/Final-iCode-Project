ComIT

ComIT is a charity which aims to develop a community that links people struggling to overcome employment barriers with companies looking for talented IT professionals. Our work targets the skills gap in the labour market by focusing on the tools and languages required by local companies, as well as on much needed soft skills and professional development capabilities. Our programs are planned, developed and executed by local professionals and volunteers working at leading companies. We believe that the best way to help people overcome employment barriers is by working as a community to provide tools to work, considering the local labor market needs.
